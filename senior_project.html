Overview:

Making robots and automated car was something that I have being dreamed since I was a child. To make the dream comes true and fulfil the requirements for my senior project, I challenged myself with something I have not done before: building a remote-controlled (RC) car. I started off with learning how the car runs electronically and how to control the car wirelessly. Then later, I tended to add more features on it: obstacles avoidance, car-cam, and heat detection. The process of learning something new was challenging but fun. I finished the project with the car worked and could detect and avoid obstacles. However, I want to add more features on it and hope these features would be added in version 2.0 or 3.0.

Features:

  • User can remotely control with PS4 controller (Dualshock4).
  • The car can detect on-coming obstacles and avoid by changing direction.

Coming Soon:

  • User can experience the view-on-car with the LED screen on the controller.
  • User can detect heat (human heat or any kind of heat sources).
